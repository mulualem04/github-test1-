Accommodation outside Addis Ababa

Paradise Lodge, Arba Minch Located in Arba Minch . The hotel enjoys a spectacular hilltop
setting overlooking Rift Valley basin. The location makes it an ideal base from which to explore
the remote villages and unspoiled cultures of the magnificent Omo Valley.

Website: www.paradiselodgeethiopia.com

Eco-Omo Lodge, Jinka A new eco-lodge located on the outskirts of Jinka town across the Neri River,
one of the tributaries to the great Omo River and near the Arie village, one of the main tribes of Jinka.
The lodge offers horseback riding, trekking in a beautiful mountains surrounding. Jinka, home-cooked meals
from their own garden, plus eco-friendly tented cabins with great views.

Website: www.eco-omo.com

Buska or Turmi Lodge, Turmi Buska Lodge overlooks the plains of and the Buska Mountain and has been
constructed to blend beautifully in to the natural surroundings.

Website: www.buskalodge.com
